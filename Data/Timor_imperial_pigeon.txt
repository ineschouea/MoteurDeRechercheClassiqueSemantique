The '''Timor imperial pigeon''' (''Ducula cineracea'') is a species of [[bird]] in the family [[Columbidae]]. It is found in [[Timor]] and [[Wetar]]. Its natural [[habitat]]s are subtropical or tropical dry [[forest]]s and subtropical or tropical moist [[montane forest]]s. It is threatened by [[habitat loss]].
