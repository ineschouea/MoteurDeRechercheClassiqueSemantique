The '''balonpesado''' is a [[team sport]], devised for both open field as closed, in which two sets of five players each try to score goals within circles drawn on the ground of each end of the field. It remains as the only team sport which is indigenous to Colombia.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.buenaventura.gov.co/images/multimedia/balon_pesado.pdf|title=Balonpesado. Versión 21 anual actualizada.|last=Lozano Batalla|first=Roberto|date=|website=|publisher=|access-date=17 August 2016}}</ref>

== History ==
The balonpesado, initially called "''Balón Mano Pesado" (''Heavy Handball in English''),'' was created on March 27, 1973 in Colombia, at the seaport of [[Buenaventura, Valle del Cauca|Buenaventura]] by Roberto Lozano Batalla,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://balonpesado.blogspot.com.co/2009/02/historia-del-balon-pesado.html|title=History of Balonpesado (Spanish)|last=|first=|date=19 February 2009|website=Historia del Balón Pesado|publisher=Comité Inter-Institucional de Educación Ambiental|access-date=17 August 2016}}</ref> a physical education teacher who worked at the time at the Industrial & Technical Institute Gerardo Valencia Cano. In 1974 the balonpesado was officially recognised by [[Coldeportes]]. To date of 2016, its practice has been extended by more than 24 [[Departments of Colombia|departments]] and 19 countries all over the globe,<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://afrocolombianosvisibles.blogspot.com.co/2010/08/roberto-lozano-batalla.html|title=Ciudadano de muchas facetas: inventor del balonpesado e historiador de Buenaventura y del Pacífico colombiano.|last=Salazar|first=Licenia|date=22 August 2010|website=Afrocolombianos Visibles|publisher=|access-date=17 August 2016}}</ref> moreover several international meetings have been presented with guest countries such as the United States, Mexico, Germany, Cuba, Israel, Chile, USS México, Alemania, Cuba, Israel, Chile, Unión Soviética, Francia, Bulgaria, entre otros.<ref name=":0" />
