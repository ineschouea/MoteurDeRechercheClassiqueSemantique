'''Force Back''' or '''Forcing Back''' is a game played by students in [[New Zealand]] and [[Australia]] at lunch or recess. It is played with a [[rugby football|rugby]] ball but can be played with a [[Association football|soccer]] ball/football as well. Some skills that are improved in this are kicking, aim, distance control, running and catching. The rules are usually modified by students themselves, depending on what environment they are playing on. The standard rules are as follow:  <br />

==Rules==

* The goal of Force Back is to kick the ball down the field towards the opposition's goal line.
* A point is scored when the ball bounces over the back line, not if it goes over on the full.
* If a player catches a kick on the full, their team can advance five paces. 
* The first team with 10 points on the board wins
